Secret Chamber

Topic: JWT Authentication

Description: You are in a cave which leads to a secret chamber. It is the headquarters of an intelligence agency. Find the secret code to enter the headquarters.

Solution: 
The application presents multiple entry points(they are all decoys):

/login – non-functional authentication

/dashboard – limited access with subtle hints

/api/status – misleading client-side hints

No JWT is stored in cookies or localStorage, suggesting authentication is handled elsewhere.
